Horcha_latte Cake Recipe

Home

Description

This horchata latte cake is soaked with additional horchata after baking. Topped with whipped topping, espresso powder. and cinnamon, it's a dirty horchata in cake form!

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, plus more for sprinkling
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract

    • Steps

    1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (or 175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
    2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon in a bowl.
    3. Beat butter in a large bowl with an electric mixer on medium for 30 seconds. Gradually beat in sugar 1/4 cup at a time. Scrape bowl. Beat until light and fluffy, 2 minutes more.
    4. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each. Beat in 4 teaspoons espresso powder, the vanilla, and almond extract.
    5. Bake and Enjoy
